**Welcome to BanditGUI!**  
Your ultimate companion for tackling Overthewire's Bandit CTF. Simplify challenges, track progress, and enhance your learning experience—all within a user-friendly interface designed to support your CTF journey.  

Welcome to the Bandit Learning Assistant! 🎮

I'm here to help you learn Linux commands and complete the Bandit wargame challenges. Feel free to:

• Ask questions about any level
• Get help with specific commands
• Request hints or explanations
• Practice in the built-in terminal

Let's start your cybersecurity journey! 🚀

---

Available Commands:
• !help    - Show this help message
• !hints   - Get hints for the current level
• !level   - Show current level information
• !next    - View next level requirements
• !solve   - Get the solution for the current level
• !reset   - Reset current level progress
• !clear   - Clear the chat history
• !about   - Show information about BanditGUI
